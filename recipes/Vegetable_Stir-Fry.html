Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok. Add garlic and ginger and saut√© for 1 minute.
  2. Add all the vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  3. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
