Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 3 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for 1 hour.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot. Fry onions until golden brown. Add garlic, ginger, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until it's done.
  5. Layer half the cooked rice over the chicken, followed by coriander and mint leaves. Repeat with remaining rice and herbs.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes until rice is fully cooked.
  7. Serve hot with raita.
