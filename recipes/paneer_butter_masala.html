Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add paneer cubes and mix well. Add cream and garam masala. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with naan or rice.
